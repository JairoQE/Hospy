DATOS A ANOTAR EN measurements.csv
----------------------------------
- period_start / period_end
- uptime_percent (del dashboard)
- total_downtime_min (estimado: (100 - uptime%) × horas × 60)
- incident_count_B (fallos visibles en Passed checks)
- RAv-2-G = total_downtime_min / incident_count_B  (si B=0: reportar 0)

CÁLCULO RÁPIDO RAv-1-G
----------------------
X = uptime_percent / 100   (ej. 99.99 % → 0.9999)

CÁLCULO RÁPIDO RAv-2-G (si 99.99 % en 7 días)
---------------------------------------------
Tiempo total ≈ 10 080 min
Downtime ≈ 0.01 % × 10 080 ≈ 1 min
Si B=1 → RAv-2-G ≈ 1 min
